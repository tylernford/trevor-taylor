About

Over the last ten years I have worked to cultivate a rich understanding of informal pedagogies and an in-depth knowledge of science exhibits as an interpretive media. I am currently investigating the relationships between staffed experiences and exhibits to better understand what kinds of experiences may occur at their intersection.

My greatest strengths lie in ideation and prototyping. I have accumulated a number of making skills over the years but am always learning more.
